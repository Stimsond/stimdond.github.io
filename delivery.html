If you want your bread delivered, you can choose to have it brought to your house, or you can choose to pick it up from us at one of our locations.

If you want your bread delivered to you, there is a $5 delivery fee, or you can pick your bread up at one of our locations for free. If you choose to have it delivered, you will recieve your bread within 1 week, however you can also choose to rush your order and recieve it within 48 hours, which will add $5.
