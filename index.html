Sourdough bread has a unique taste that isn't matched by any other food, and is fluffy and delicious. We started making sourdough in 2020, when the pandemic started and we had more time at home. We decided to try making a new kind of bread, as we wanted to eat more of it without going to the store as much. We were lucky enough to recieve a sourdough starter from an established german baker. That same sourdough lives on in our kitchen and is the base of all of our breads. After 3 years, we have perfected the flavor and technique. We are now taking the next step and starting to sell our breads to people in our community. We currently only sell it online. If you would like to try some, we have many different flavors, and different sizes. If you are interested in seeing what we offer, click here, and if you would like to order some click here.
