The base of our bread is made out of only three ingredients: water, flour, and salt. From there we add in the other ingredients that make the bread special and add the different flavors to it. Our sourdough is very old, as we recieved it from an established German baker a few years ago. The same sourdough starter lives on in our kitchen, and is in every loaf of bread we make.

All of our bread is dairy free, except for our garlic-parmesean bread. If you have questions about our ingredients or any allergies you may have, don't hesitate to reach out.
